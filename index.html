What we are doing…

We are an interdisciplinary research group at Department of Bridge Engineering, College of Civil Engineering, Tongji University.

Reasearch Fields:

  1. Design theory and method of long-span bridges.
  2. Structural system of long-span bridges.
  3. Computer program for structural analysis of long-span bridges.
  4. Bridge management system and engineering control.

Applications: Our research has faciliated the design, construction and maintenance of many long-span bridges, for instance, the Sutong Yangtze River Bridge, the Lupu Yangtze River Bridge, the Huangpu Pearl River Bridge and etc.

Prof. Xiao was invited to give a keynote speech

Rucheng Xiao
Mar 21, 2023

Prof. Xiao was invited to give a keynote speech at the Second World Conference on Long Span Arch Bridge Construction Technology held from March 19 – 21 in Nanning, GuangXi Zhuang Autonomous Region, China.

The presentation was titled “Past and Present of Arch Bridges — Also Discussing Innovations of Arch Bridges”. From the Corbel arch over 4000 years ago to the stone arch bridge of the Mycenaean Civilization, from the ancient arch bridge of the Rome period to the Bayonne Bridge, the brilliance of the arch bridge in the past is shown. Then, from several points of view, including the development of bridge span and application of new material, the structural system and key structural member, the construction method, the calculation theory and analysis method, the innovation of the arch bridge after World War II is presented. It is revealed that only innovation can help to achieve longer spans and diverse structural forms of arch bridges. Possible directions for the future development of arch bridges are discussed in the last part.

Rucheng Xiao
Professor of Tongji University

My research interests include bridge structural system, bridge design theory and method, bridge management system.
